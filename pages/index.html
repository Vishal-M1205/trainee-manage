Training Management System

Manage your training plans with ease.

Features

Assign training programs to employees based on their department, role, or skill requirements. Administrators can easily manage enrollments and ensure employees receive the appropriate training at the right time.

Monitor employee training status in real time. View course completion percentages, pending modules, and overall participation to ensure learning objectives are being met.

Organize training sessions by managing trainers, schedules, course durations, and training calendars. This helps streamline planning and avoid scheduling conflicts.

Generate training reports and track employee certifications upon course completion. Detailed insights help management evaluate performance and maintain compliance requirements.
